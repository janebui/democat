About Us

We are a small team based in California. We believe in the power of voting, but we know that navigating through and researching candidates on the ballot can be a tedious, time-consuming task. We wanted to make voting faster and easier for like-minded individuals, and that’s how Democat was born.

We support:

  • Environmental Protections, Clean Energy, & Climate Action
  • Universal Healthcare
  • Wealth Equality
  • Black Lives Matter
  • Minority Rights
  • Women's Rights
  • LGBTQ Rights
  • Gun Violence Prevention
  • Science & Education

Thank you for considering the Democat’s voting guide to assist you in your election decisions!
